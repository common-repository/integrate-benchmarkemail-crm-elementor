Benchmark Email Integration for Elementor Pro allow you to send your Elementor Pro Form Widget entries directly to your Benchmark Email account

== Description ==

<p>Benchmark Email Integration for Elementor Pro Form Widget allow you to send your Elementor Pro Form Widget forms entries directly
to your Benchmark Email account.

You can add unlimited elementor forms with different contact lists from your Benchmark account, choose your custom fields and map them to the form fields with ease using Instant showing of the fields without any refreshing of the page.</p>
 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u0qe94wtA8g

Features:
<ul>
<li>Integrate your Elementor Pro Form Widget forms with
Benchmark Email Account.</li>
<li>Ease of mapping custom fields to the form fields</li>
<li>Load custom fields from each list with ease.</li>
<li>Integrate unlimited Elementor Pro Form Widget forms.</li>
<li>Choose from more than 20 custom fields.</li>
<li>Super easy to set-up!</li>
<li>Instant showing of the fields of each list without any
refreshing of the page</li>
</ul>

Integration Notes:
- This is an integration with BenchmarkEmail CRM
- Terms of service https://www.benchmarkemail.com/terms-of-use/
